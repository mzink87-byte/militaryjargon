About Military Jargon

The Problem

Military briefings are full of acronyms. Half the time you’re decoding instead of listening, and you feel like you’re missing critical information because you don’t understand the terms. Most acronym sites online are wrong, outdated, or locked behind a CAC.

The Solution

I built this site to fix that. Fast definitions. Plain language. No logins. Built by a Soldier, for Soldiers.

What We Offer

  • 11,000+ terms across all five branches — Army, Navy, Air Force, Marines, and Coast Guard
  • Every job code: Army MOS, Marine MOS, Navy ratings & NEC, and Air Force AFSC
  • Acronyms, slang, drill & ceremony, doctrine publications, installations, Navy ships, awards, and modern programs (AFT, NGSW, NGC2, and more)
  • Instant search as you type — works on your phone in the field
  • Source-linked: references click straight through to Army Pubs and official manuals
  • New terms added regularly

The Mission

Make military jargon clear and accessible for everyone — new Soldiers, veterans, spouses, or anyone trying to translate.

Part of JargonHubs

Military Jargon is part of the JargonHubs network — more sites are coming soon for aviation, medical, finance, and other fields.
